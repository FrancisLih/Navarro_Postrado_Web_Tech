Management Information Systems Department

Philosophy and Objectives

ABOUT THE OFFICE

The Management Information System Department's major areas of responsibility include information system and technical services

VISION

We envision the MIS department to spearhead in providing Information technology services to its clientele to realize the Colegio's Vision.

MISSION

The MIS department provides forefront in the application of Information Technology resources by collaborations, innovations, accountability and delivering its services in a cost-effective, timely and professional manner that supports the mission of the Colegio.

QUALITY OBJECTIVES
  1. Maintain reliable, up-to-date appropriate access to technological infrastructures to meet changing service demands
  2. Continuous professional development.
