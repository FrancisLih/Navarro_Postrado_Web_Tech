Religious Education Department

Philosophy and Objectives

ABOUT THE OFFICE

The department serves as the academic unit of the division that runs the formal mode of religious instruction

VISION

We envision to establish Letran Calamba as the center of Religious Education in the Region IV-A area.

MISSION

We, guided by the Dominican philosophy of education and spirituality, commit ourselves to teach religious education in the light of the three guiding principles: (1) to share the basic Christian concepts based on given contexts, (2) to promote an inter -cultural religious education that is operational, and (3) to advocate a praxis-oriented religious instruction.

QUALITY OBJECTIVES
  1. To develop an integral Religious Education/Christian Living Education of the Colegio.
  2. To collaborate with other departments that foster the spiritual growth of the community, and engage in community extension services, culture responsive, and formative programs.
  3. To provide students with integral Christian formation founded on the Scriptures, Church teachings, and Traditions
  4. To develop Letran Calamba as a center for Religious Education in Region IV-A area.
