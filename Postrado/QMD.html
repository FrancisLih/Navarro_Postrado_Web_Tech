Quality Management Department

Philosophy and Objectives

ABOUT THE OFFICE

The Quality Management Department's primary goals are to oversee and promote the institutional accreditation and certification of the Colegio.

VISION

The Quality Management Department (QMD) envisions Letran Calamba as a benchmark in educational quality management system in Laguna and as the management arm of the Colegio in establishing and nurturing a culture of accountability and continuous improvement to provide quality service to its stakeholders.

MISSION

To uphold Letran Calamba's quality policy and to support its mission and vision, the Quality Management Department commits itself in promoting and providing support for accreditation and certification and in achieving and maintaining high quality educational standards and services through application of an effective quality management spreading

QUALITY OBJECTIVES
  1. To ensure organizational efficiency and continuous improvement through accreditation and certification
  2. To ensure the compliance of departments on the approved updates of the quality management system
  3. To deliver quality services that would result to customer desirability
