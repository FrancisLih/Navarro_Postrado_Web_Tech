Student Services Department

Philosophy and Objectives

ABOUT THE OFFICE

The Student Affairs and Development Department is mandated to provide student-centered learning environment which promotes education with leadership and life success skills to empower them with sharp mind and strong body with Filipino, Dominican and Christian ideals and values.

VISION

We envision ourselves to be highly regarded for our quality, efficient, and seamless services and to provide state of the art sports facilities, center of career development and leadership programs to serve as hallmark of student welfare and development exemplifying best practices in the region through an integrative campus community sharing.

MISSION

We commit to continuously improve student-centered learning environment, promote education beyond books, equip students with life success skills and empower them with sharp mind and strong body for good disposition in life to promote a community of professionals honed in Filipino, Dominican and Christian ideals and values.

QUALITY OBJECTIVES
  1. To continually enhance the quality of students’ life by providing them various developmental learning programs, established system and procedures and student welfare services.
  2. To contribute to the total human development of Letran Community members by providing a culture sensitive, faith-based, socially responsive and integrative environment through leadership skills development, innovative and integrated counseling programs and wellness programs guided by Dominican principles and recognized ethical standard.
  3. To provide student centered activities to complement quality focused, industry-aligned and competency-based academic instruction for integral development of students as productive contributors in nation building and glocally competitive industry leaders.
  4. To establish student services and programs which are regionally, nationally and internationally competitive and recognized.
