Admissions, Student Assistance and Scholarship Office

Philosophy and Objectives

ABOUT THE OFFICE

The Admissions, Student Assistance and Scholarship Office caters the application in the Basic Education to collegiate levels of prospective students who meet the required admission credentials and facilitates their admissions in the Colegio as well as assess and grant scholarships to academically qualified but financially challenges individuals.

VISION

We envision ourselves to become effective contributor in the promotion of best professional practices and services related to admissions and student scholarships.

MISSION

The mission of the Admissions, Student Assistance and Scholarship Office is to recruit, admit, and serve an eligible and diverse student population. It is commitment to attain the quality of students by ensuring efficient and systematic evaluation of the admission process. Through multiple recruitment strategies, the Office also works to ensure that academically qualified but financially challenged students have access to higher education through the scholarship programs being offered by the Colegio as well as to deliver effective services for student welfare and development.

QUALITY OBJECTIVES
  1. To continually enhance the quality of students' life by providing them various developmental learning programs, established system and procedures and student welfare services.
  2. To contribute to the total human development of Letran Community members by providing a culture sensitive, faith-based, socially responsive and integrative environment through skills development, support services and values formation programs.
  3. To develop and maintain the efficient and effective implementation of recruitment programs, projects and activities geared towards the attainment of the office vision, mission and objective.
