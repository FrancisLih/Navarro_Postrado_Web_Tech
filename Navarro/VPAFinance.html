Colegio De San Juan de Letran Calamba
Office of the Vice President for Finance

Philosophy and Objectives

ABOUT THE OFFICE

The Office of the Vice President Finance is tasked to safeguard the financial interests of the Colegio.

VISION

The Finance Division envisions itself to be an effective and efficient generator of revenues, innovative creators of resources, and most vigilant guardians of reserves.

MISSION

The Finance Division commits itself in safeguarding the financial interest of the Colegio by constantly striving to manage costs.

QUALITY OBJECTIVES
  1. To maintain reasonable amount of returns for each academic year.
  2. To maintain a positive cash flow for each operating period while ensuring that academic requirements are sustained.
  3. To provide timely, accurate and relevant information for operations review and decision-making purposes.
